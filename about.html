About Us

In 2013, FishTastic was founded by Ms V. Sithara of JCU, together with the co-sponsor of Singapore Sea Aquarium. FishTastic sells a variety of fishes prices ranging from affordable to luxury. FishTastic also carries products and accessories and fish foods of many well-known brands like Hikari, Tropical, and Rio. Our Partners

We have trading partners worldwide, whom we have business dealing with in countries all over the world. We continue to explore and look for trading partners in other parts of the world, so as to acquire more products to develop our farm into a one-stop shopping paradise for all fish lovers.

Come and visit our store to make your marine aquarium beautiful and pristine. We are here to share our knowledge and make your hobby as easy as possible. We believe that it is truly a privilege to be able to look at a part of the ocean everyday.

If you have any questions, please feel free to contact us by phone or email.

Mission & Vision

Our goal is to diversify the delights and pleasure of fishkeeping to the society. To be the number one fish aquarium in the global market. Be the best that we want to be.
