Goals

FishTastic, a fish shop in Singapore brings to you the best fish experience in the island. With the newly opened pet shop, FishTastic hopes to raise the bar even further in your pet experience, bringing you dogs, cats and guinea pigs. Newly launched events such as the upcoming landscaping exhibition can gather the community together, and spark up a new light in making your place look and feel good for you and your pet.

With the launch of the website, we plan to push things even further such as providing our customers with better services for caring their pets. The launch of our very own veterinary clinic is a solid example of just that with doctors standing by for your every need. We also showcase the various treats that you can buy for your pet that is healthy and nutritious. The website also allows allows everyone to join in free events that we host in which they may be interested in and know the other services that happens every month.

Our mission is to provide you with the best fish and pet experience possible. With the help of the website, we are able to do just that. Care for your pet is at our top priority, so we provide you with the best veterinary services possible and host events such as a talk with the Dog Whisperer that can help you and all other pet owners around become a good master for their pet.
